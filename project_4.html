Twitter Alert: Tracking Disasters with Social Media

With the growing volume of people using social media on a daily basis, could Twitter be leveraged to crowdsource information about a rapid-onset disaster, especially when traditional methods of communication fail?

I worked with a team to train a model on pre- and post-disaster tweets to detect shifts in trends under emergency situations and developed a prototype for filtering real-time tweets based on emergency-related words.

Tools & Techniques Used

  • Data Extraction: BeautifulSoup, Selenium
  • Natural Language Processing: NLTK
  • Classification Modeling: Scikit-learn
  • APIs: Twitter
  • Geolocation Lookup: GeoNames
