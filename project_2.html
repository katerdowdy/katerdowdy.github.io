Men's Rights & Feminism: Search for Common Ground

Online communities have done as much to bring people together as they have to polarize the public discourse on everything from the platforms of our politicans to the color of Starbucks' coffee cups. But if we take a closer look, do we disagree as much as we think?

Using natural language processing and Pushshift's API, I attempted to explore two Subreddit communities with diverging attitudes about feminism and gender equality. Are Men's Rights Activists and Feminists worlds apart, or is there room for common ground?

Tools & Techniques Used

  • API: Pushshift (Reddit)
  • Natural Language Processing: NLTK
  • Classification Modeling: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Naive Bayes, Support Vector Classifier
